What is Trezór Bridge?

Trezór Bridge is an essential communication tool that establishes a secure connection between your Trezór hardware wallet and web browser. It enables seamless interaction with Trezór Suite and Trezór Wallet while maintaining the highest security standards for your cryptocurrency transactions.

Why You Need It

Bridge serves as a crucial security layer, ensuring all communications between your device and computer remain encrypted and protected. It handles device discovery, communication protocols, and firmware updates while preventing unauthorized access attempts.

Easy Setup

Installation is straightforward - download, install, and you're ready to go. Trezór Bridge runs quietly in the background, automatically managing connections and updates without requiring user intervention. Compatible with all major operating systems.

Enhanced Security

Every connection through Trezór Bridge is encrypted and verified, preventing man-in-the-middle attacks and ensuring your private keys never leave your device. Regular security updates maintain protection against emerging threats.
